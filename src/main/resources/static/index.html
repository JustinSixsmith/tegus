About Us

We are passionate about tegu lizards and want to share that passion with others. We have been breeding and caring for tegus for many years and have a wealth of knowledge and experience to share with our customers. Our goal is to provide healthy, happy tegus to loving homes, and to support our customers every step of the way as they care for their new pets. Whether you're a seasoned reptile enthusiast or a first-time owner, we're here to help you every step of the way.

Visit our facility to meet our tegus, learn about their care and feeding, and find the perfect pet for you. Our knowledgeable staff will answer any questions you may have and provide you with all the information and supplies you need to care for your new pet.
